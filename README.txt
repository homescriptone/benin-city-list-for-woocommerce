<p>A better way to send SMS from WooCommerce Store to your customers.</p>

== Description ==

<blockquote>
<p>To use this plugin you need to open an account on <a href="https://twilio.com" rel="follow">Twilio</a> or <a href="https://telesign.com" rel="follow">Telesign</a> and have SMS credits in your account.</p>
</blockquote>
<p>This plugin allow you to send SMS Notifications to your customers from your store through SMS Services : <strong>Twilio</strong> and <strong>Telesign</strong>.</p>

<p>Twilio and Telesign is two of the best solutions on the market for delivering text functionnality to your website or applications.</p>
<p>The plugin allows to automatically send an SMS to the customer and owner of the shop after a purchase.</p>
<p>You can also send your next promotions by SMS by defining the messages to send to your customers in the settings of the plugin.</p>
<p>By default, once configured it send a sms after a order is passed on your shop.</p>

<h2>Plugin Features</h2>
<br>
<ul>
<li>Get notified by <strong>SMS </strong> as soon as customers buy on your shop.</li>
<li>Preconfigured tags is available for sending your <strong>sms notifications</strong> easily.</li>
<li>Notify your customers by <strong>SMS </strong> as soon as they buy on your website.</li>
<li>Possibility to <strong>send message</strong> if shop manager change order status.</li>
<li>Sending <strong>bulk sms</strong> to a customer list that you have created yourself.</li>
<li>An export utility is available to export your customers name and phone number in <strong>CSV format</strong>.</li>
</ul>

== Installation ==
<h4>Automatic installation</h4>
<p>Automatic installation is the easiest option as WordPress handles the file transfers itself and you do not need to leave your web browser. to
do an automatic install of, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu and click Add New.

In the search field type "Ultimate SMS Notifications for WooCommerce" and click Search Plugins. Once you've found out about this plugin, click here. Most importantly, you can install it by simply clicking "Install Now".</p>
<h4>Manual installation</h4>
<p>The manual installation method involves downloading our plugin and uploading it to your web server via your favorite FTP application. The WordPress codex contains <a href="https://codex.wordpress.org/Managing_Plugins#Manual_Plugin_Installation" rel="follow">instructions on how to do this here</a>.</p>
